Projects

SIAGA DIKTI

SIAGA (Sistem Informasi Kelembagaan) is a project owned by Indonesian Ministry of Education and Research (Kementrian Pendidikan, Kebudayaan, Riset dan Teknologi/Kemdikbudristek) via Higher Education Directorate (Direktorat Jendral Pendidikan Tinggi/Ditjen Dikti). SIAGA provides multiple administrative services for institutions and organizations of higher education (College/Universities). I worked on several redesigns of the landing page. I also worked on several core features such as the ticket tracking system, multiple admin evaluation modules, and several APIs integrations.

Carolus - Isoman

The isoman by Rs. Carolus is a tracking and monitoring app intended for COVID-19 patients who are on a self-quaratine (isolasi mandiri/isoman). Other than providing patient monitoring, this app also provides administration tools (such as doctor/bed management for COVID-19) for Rs. Carolus. I worked on various modules and features of this app.
